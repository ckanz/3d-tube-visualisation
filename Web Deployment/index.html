3D London Underground Visualisation

3D map of the London Underground generated with each station's GPS data and depth/height below/above ground level

Depth and Height scaled to show differences - Height: above sea level; Depth: height above ordnance datum;

Transparent circles represent the station's "busyness" (Amount of annual entries and exits)

_

Use right mouse button to look around

Use mouse wheel (or W and S key) to zoom in/out

Use arrow keys to move around
